Strategy Design Social

Launching Desert Rose Australia in a high-trust beauty category

Pre-launch → Market entry → Growth

Desert Rose Australia launched into one of the most competitive and tightly regulated categories in beauty: SPF skincare. Founded by Jessica Mauboy, the brand required a digital presence that could balance founder visibility, scientific credibility, and cultural integrity from day one.

I led the creative rollout beyond social media — building a brand people could trust, connect with, and want to be part of.

Lifecycle Design Systems

Designing the system behind Prezzee's scalable lifecycle marketing

Design System → Email → Performance

At Prezzee, every lifecycle email is a touchpoint in someone's gifting journey. As lifecycle matured, it needed stronger design infrastructure. I rebuilt a modular component library in Figma and Stripo — a flexible system of reusable building blocks where email data informed layout logic, CTA placement, and A/B testing flexibility.

Every component was designed for both brand expression and measurable performance, resulting in an average of 123% increase in clicks and 140% increase in unique opens.

Web Design Brand

Elevating pet care brand Kare Kanine through digital design

Brand → Web Rebuild → SEO

The goal was to create a polished, trustworthy digital presence that clearly communicated the breadth of services — from grooming and walking to pet sitting, wedding pet care, and drop-in visits.

A responsive website rebuild, SEO optimisation, and brand identity refinement focused on elevating both usability and brand perception.

Social Design E-commerce

Shaping wellness brand Bloomi Tea for life on the go

Creative Direction → Social → E-Commerce

Bloomi Tea is a premium wellness brand redefining tea for life on the go. The challenge was to translate a highly functional product into a lifestyle-led digital presence that felt elevated, joyful, and clear.

Through creative direction across social and e-commerce, we established how Bloomi shows up online through playful, aspirational content that balances education with delight.
